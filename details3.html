Spiti Valley

Days: 8 Nights/9 Days



Day 1: Chandigarh - Shimla

Arrive at Chandigarh Airport, transfer to Shimla, Check in the hotel, and rest of the day sightseeing in Shimla. Dinner and Overnight Stay in Shimla.

Day 2: Shimla - Kufri - Shimla

Morning after breakfast go for a day excursion Kufri & Mashobra sightseeing, Evening back to hotel dinner & overnight stay at Hotel.

Day 3: Shimla - Narkanda - Rampur Bushhar/Saharan

This day, post breakfast we start our Journey towards Rampur Bushahr. Almost to the end of the Spiti valley, you will start seeing more settlements today. The latter part of our journey will see the Sutlej River giving us company till we reach Pooh. Dinner & Overnight Stay in Sarahan/Rampur Bushhar. Saharan/Rampur Bushhar both used to be capital for Princely state of Rampur Bushhar.

Day 4: Rampur Bushhar/Saharan - Sangla - Chitkul

After Breakfast, Explore around Sarahan and drive to Chitkul. Dinner & Overnight Stay in Chitkul.

Day 5: Chitkul - Kalpa -Nako

During winters, the place mostly remains covered with snow and the inhabitants move to lower regions of Himachal. Potatoes grown at Chitkul are one of the best in the world and are very costly. After we reach Kalpa, we will also visit Kamru Fort. After reaching Nako we will visit Nako Lake and Nako Monastery. Dinner and Overnight in Nako

Day 6: Nako - Sumdo - Tabo - Kaza

We start early towards Kaza. On our way to Kaza, we will visit Chango Gompa to see the prayer wheel which measures about 3.5 meters (11 ft) in diameter and is made up of Yak Skin. Also, it is older than 500 Years. Then we visit the Dhankar Palace which was the capital of the Spiti Valley Kingdom in the 17th Century. Dinner & Overnight Stay in Kaza.

Day 7: Kaza - Pin Valley - Kaza

After Breakfast, we drive to Pin Valley, Pin Valley National Park is a National park of India located in the Spiti Valley in the Lahaul and Spiti district, in the state of Himachal Pradesh. It is located in far northern India. and return back to Kaza. Dinner and Overnight Stay in Kaza.

Day 8: Kaza - Kibber - Kaza

Early Breakfast, drive towards Kibber aka Kyibar village rests high in the Spiti Valley in Himachal Pradesh at an altitude of 14200 ft. It is famous for several things -a monastery, Kibber Wildlife Sanctuary, the highest post office in the world, and as a destination with the highest motorable road. Reach Kaza till evening and for the rest of the day in leisure. Dinner and Overnight Stay in Kaza.

Day 9 : Kaza - Kunzum La - Batal -Chandratal

Chandratal or Chandra Tal or Chandra Taal or simply Moon Lake is a barren but beautiful lake located at a height of 14100 feet in Himachal Pradesh in northern India. It comes under the Spiti and Lahaul district of Himachal Pradesh and is situated at a distance of six km from Kumjjum Pass. Dinner and Overnight Stay in the Hotel.

Day 10 : Chandratal - Batal - Manali (Via Atal Tunnel or Rohtang La)

After Breakfast, Drive to Manali, check in the Hotel. If time permits, go for local sightseeing- Hadimba Temple, Mall Road Van Vihar, Club House, Etc. if you want to explore anything more then do it as per your wish. Dinner and Overnight Stay in Manali

Day 11 : Manali Local - Solang Valley - Mandi

Morning after a scrumptious breakfast drive for a full day trip to Solang Adventure Valley where you can admire the beautiful sights of the surroundings and can enjoy numerous adventure activities like ropeway, zorbing, Paragliding, etc. at your own cost. While in the winter, the same Solang Valley takes the shape of a Snowy Blanket, thus forming a perfect vista for several snow activities like skiing and snow scooter. While returning you can visit Him valley Amusement & Cultural Park, After enjoying the day outing transfer to Mandi. Overnight stay in Mandi. We do this journey if order to save our time the next day.

Day 12 : Mandi - Chandigarh

After Early Breakfast we will transfer to Chandigarh. Upon your arrival in Chandigarh go for Local Sightseeing - Rose Garden, Rock Garden & Sukhna Lake, etc. Evening to Chandigarh Airport Drop.
