Kashmir

Days: 8 Nights/9 Days

Day 1: Arrival at Srinagar

Pickup from Srinagar Airport we will transfer to Hotel and explore the nearby area & overnight stay in hotel.

Day 2: Srinagar Local Sightseeing

After an early breakfast we will proceed for Shankra achariyaTemple (The Shankaracharya Temple is also known as the Jyeshteshwara Temple. It is situated on top of the Shankaracharya Hill. It is dedicated to Lord Shiva) , Nishant Garden (Nishant Bagh happens to be an enchanting twelve terraced garden, packed with green trees, scented flowers, and beautiful fountains) , other Mugal garden etc. we will return back to our hotel in the late evening and enjoy a comfortable overnight stay.

Day 3: Srinagar - Sonmarg - Srinagar

After breakfast we travel to Sonamarg, after Reaching We will visit beautiful place in laps of Himalayas snow point at sonamarg Sonmarg gives rise to The Three Sisters (Kashmir). In its vicinity lies the great Himalayan glaciers of Kashmir Valley,of above 5,000 m , zero point etc. (own cost) we will return back to our hotel in the late evening and enjoy a comfortable overnight stay.

Day 4: Srinagar - Gulmarg

After an amazing overnight stay at hotel. In morning having your breakfast we will proceed to Gulmarg. And spend whole day in the laps of Himalayan ranges (gulmarg) and Relaxing overnight stay in there.

Day 5: Gulmarg-Gandola

After an amazing overnight stay at hotel. In morning having your breakfast we will proceed to Gulmarg from Srinagar (60km). After reaching we will visit mesmerizing Gondola snow point so you can enjoy the sking and other snow activities (at your own cost) And spend Relaxing overnight in hotel there.

Day 6: Gulmarg - Pahalgam

In morning having your Aamzing breakfast we will proceed to Pahalgam. In evening enjoy the magnificent view from your room and spend comfortable night stay in there.

Day 7: Pahalgam Local Sightseeing

After delicious breakfast we will drop you till the taxi stand so from there you can take a local taxi for sightseeing(at your own cost) in beautiful villages, Chandanvadi , Aaru Valley , Betab Valley etc. In evening you can visit the mini Switzerland by horse riding (at own cost). Relaxing overnight stay in here. (private vehicles are not allowed from taxi stand)

Day 8: Pahalgam - Srinagar Houseboat.

We will transfer to House boat at Dal lake and enjoy shikara ride. In evening we can enjoy the mesmerizing view from your house boat's balcony and overnight stay there.

Day 9 : Srinagar Airport drop

After a late morning breakfast, we will drive to the airport for your flight home.Our Service will end there.

Cost Includes:

  • Arrival at Srinagar airport and drop also here.
  • Double sharing room in hotel
  • 08 Nights/09 Days accommodation as given above.
  • All Transfers & Sightseeing By tempo traveler/Car which is depends on the number of members in group.
  • Meals as mentioned in itinerary Plan: - Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner from our Separate kitchen.
  • All currently applicable taxes i.e. Hotel Tax & Transportation.

Cost Does Not Include

  • Any airfare, train fare, overland journey which is not mentioned in the itinerary.
  • Gandola tickets
  • Sonamarg Zero point
  • Horse ride Pahalgam
  • Guide & Entrance fees during sightseeing
  • Room Heater Charge
