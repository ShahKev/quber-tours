Himachal Pradesh

Days: 11 Days/10 Nights








Day 1: Arrive Agartala

After check-in we move out to visit Agartala's famous Ujjayanta palace. Most of you may not know but this is the land from where we got our musical legends S.D Burman and R.D Burman. This father and son duo is from the Royal Family of Agartala. Overnight in Hotel

Day 2: Agartala Exploration

We explore more places in Agartala. Tripura is a place of Tribal mountain culture mixed with Bengali culture. We enjoy this cultural fusion and stay overnight in Agartala. Today we will cover Sepahijala Wildlife Sanctuary located approx 20 Km from the city and home of endemic wildlife. Neer Mahal which is a summer palace similar to the palaces in Udaipur. It is standing in the middle of Rudrasagar lake. Visit to Bangladesh border in the evening. Overnight stay at Agartala

Day 3: Agartala -Silchar

Today we proceed to Silchar - Assam's second largest city.

Day 4: Silchar - Aizawl

Today we proceed to Aizawl - the scenic capital of Mizoram. Upon arrival, we will visit Aizawl Zoo. The road condition is not great but the route is very scenic.

Day 5: Aizawl

Today we explore the city of Aizawl, we visit Mizoram State museum which accommodates several items that unravel the cultural heritage of the state and Soloman Temple - the biggest church in Mizoram

Day 6 : Aizawl - Fly Out - Guwahati

After breakfast we will go to Aizawl airport then fly to Guwahati. Its a short journey and after that we will check into a hotel. Evening we will visit Guwahati Market Overnight Stay in guwahati.

Day 7 : Guwahati - Shillong

Today we drove to Shillong. It's a journey of approx 90-100 Km. Check-in hotel, get fresh and explore Shillong town. Shillong has rightly earned the sobriquet of & Scotland of the East & owing to the rolling hills, beautiful scenery and climate. On the way you will visit the beautiful Umiam Lake, a major tourist attraction located just before entering Shillong, you can visit the local market and visit Don Bosco Monument which is a 7 storey monument where you can have a glimpse of culture and tradition of North-East, Ward's Lake where you can enjoy boating, Botanical Garden and Lady Hydari Park. Check-in the Hotel and rest of the day in leisure.

Day 8: Shillong - Cherrapunjee - Mawlynnong (Living Roots Bridge) - Shillong

In the morning you will visit Shillong peak and get a bird's eye view of the entire city of Shillong and then go to Elephanta falls. Then drive to Cherrapunjee, the erstwhile rainiest place in the world. On the way, experience the beautiful rolling hills, the flora and the waterfalls, most importantly the Nohkalikai or Seven Sisters Falls, a seven segmented waterfall which falls from a height of over a 1000 ft, you can also some of the caves like Mawsmai Cave, Arwah Cave. We also visit Living roots bridge at Mawlynnong. But please note that this visit requires a little bit of easy trekking. Those who do not want to trek down the hill can stay near the vehicle and enjoy the scenic view around. Then drive back to Shillong. Overnight stay at Shillong.

Day 9:Shillong - Guwahati

After breakfast leave for Guwahati.Check-in hotel, get fresh and then leave for Maa Kamakhya Temple, one of the famous shakti peethas located at the top of Nilachal Hills from where you can have an eagle eye view of the whole city. Then visit Umananda Temple which is located at the smallest river island of Asia. Then visit the War memorial at Dighali Pukhuri. Then in the evening you are free to visit the local market. Visit to Srimanta Sankardev Kalakshetra where you can have a view of culture and tradition of Assam. This will be an optional visit depending on the time availability. Overnight stay at Guwahati.

Day 10: Fly Out

Early morning breakfast, Fly to home from Guwahati with sweet memories!!!
